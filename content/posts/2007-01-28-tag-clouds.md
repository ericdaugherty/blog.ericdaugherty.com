<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tag_Cloud">Tag Clouds</a> are a 'new' way to display information.  Specifically, it is a visual way to depict the occurrences or popularity of a specific word.  Apparently this started with <a href="http://www.flickr.com/">Flickr</a>, although it is now common, especially among Web 2.0 companies.

I really dislike tag clouds.  OK, they aren't really offensive, but I'm a numbers guy, and I'd much rather see the information displayed in a table or graph than as a tag cloud.  I don't find it really accurate or useful.

Like any good fad, they are now being applied to everything you can think of.  For example, a <a href="http://chir.ag/phernalia/preztags/">tag cloud of presidential speeches</a>.
