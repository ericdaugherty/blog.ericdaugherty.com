I got to visit Estes Park and Rocky Mountain National Park this weekend, which gave me a chance to see some of the flood damage from this fall. &nbsp;Nothing was quite as impressive as the amount of material deposited at the base of the Alluvial Fan in RMNP. &nbsp;The river used to run down the mountain and under the bridge. &nbsp;The bridge survived, but as you can see, it no longer crosses the river. &nbsp;The river used to flow about 15 feet under this bridge. &nbsp;Now there is rock and sand above the level of the bridge...<br />
