There were a lot of naysayers when Microsoft announced their new ad campaign with Jerry Seinfeld.  Now that the <a href="http://www.microsoft.com/windows/#">first ad is out</a>, I have to say they were right.  What is this about?<br /><br />This is their rebuttal to the Apple ads?  The Apple ads were more style than substance maybe, but they all had a specific point to make that differentiated their products from Microsoft.<br /><br />And to top it off, the Flash Player (what, no Silverlight?) they used didn't allow you to pause/restart the ad.  Annoying.  The ads have been posted on YouTube several times and taken down just as quickly.  I did a quick search on YouTube and found several (real) Apple ads.  You are paying big bucks to get your message out, why are you having it taken down from a major video distributor?<br /><br />Microsoft is the big kid in the room that just can't figure out why the other kids don't think he's cool.<br /><br />All that aside, I'm typing this post on <a href="http://www.ericdaugherty.com/blog/2008/07/vista.html">Vista which as I said before, is a fine OS</a>.  Apple did a masterful job of changing the market's perception of Vista, which Microsoft did nothing to combat.  And this ad doesn't change it one bit.
