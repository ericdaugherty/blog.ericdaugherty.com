I was surprised to get an email from Netflix today notifying me that a new disc shipped out.  Netflix does not normally process movies on Saturday, so mailing a movie back on Friday means you have to wait until Tuesday to get the replacement.<div>
</div><div>I did a quick search, and indeed, it looks like <a href="http://www.examiner.com/x-8310-Trendy-Living-Examiner~y2009m6d18-Netflix-lovers-rejoice-over-Saturday-processing">Netflix is introducing Saturday processing</a> across the US.
<div>
</div><div>A great service just got better.</div></div>
