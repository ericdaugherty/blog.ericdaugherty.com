Season 6 of The Shield started today on FX.  The Shield has consistently been an amazing show, and season 6 appears to be no exception.  At the end of the (extended) season premier, I found myself wanting more, more, more.<br /><br />Anyone who isn't watching this show really should go back and enjoy the entire run.  Find the DVDs and enjoy.  This show demonstrates what TV should be.  Unfortunately shows of this quality  only seems to be found on HBO, FX, and a few other examples (Battlestar Galactica on Sci-Fi).
