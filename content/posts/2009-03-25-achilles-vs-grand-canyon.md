No, I didn't hike to the bottom.<br /><br />But, after my <a href="http://www.ericdaugherty.com/blog/2008/11/surgery.html">Achilles surgery</a> this past November I was very excited to get a real hike in.  I finished my physical therapy/rehab a few weeks ago and I am cleared for most activities that don't involve jumping.<br /><br />We started at the South Rim and hiked down 3 miles on the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bright_Angel_Trail">Bright Angel Trail</a>.  3 Miles may not seem like a lot (6 round trip), but it was also a 2,100 ft. drop (and climb) in elevation.
