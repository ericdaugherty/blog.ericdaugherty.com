Due to a series of scheduling conflicts and rain, I have not played soccer in a month.  I'm very unhappy about this.<br /><br />Soccer is my primary workout, so I haven't had much physical exercise for the last month.  The combination of this, and some heckling from my wife (she bet me I couldn't), prompted me to try to run 5 miles today.<br /><br />Well, I did it.  52 minutes, so just a bit over 10 minute miles.  Not too bad I guess.  But I'm sore.  My heel hurts (I have a bone spur), my knee hurts, and my hips hurt.  I won't be running again for a while.<br /><br />I hate running.
