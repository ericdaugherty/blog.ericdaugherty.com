USA Pro Cycling Challenge brought pro cycling to Colorado, including Golden. &nbsp;I headed out to take in the race and capture a few pictures. &nbsp;You can see all of my pictures <a href="http://www.smugmug.com/gallery/18763831_kQqX5j">here</a>.<br />
<br />
This was my first time shooting a cycling race, and I learned a lot about what works and what doesn't.  I'm already looking forward to next year's race.<br />
