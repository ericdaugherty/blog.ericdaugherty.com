I still don't 'get' <a href="http://www.twitter.com/">Twitter</a>, but I did subscribe to <a href="http://www.merlinmann.com/">Merlin Mann's</a> Twitter <a href="http://twitter.com/statuses/user_timeline/749863.rss">RSS feed</a>, and I have to say, I'm enjoying it.  Here is today's:<br /><br />"Every time you sniff and say somebody has "too much free time," the part of you that used to love making things for pure joy dies a little."<br /><br />Some of my recent favorites:<br /><br />"SEO advice that doesn't begin with "1. Don't rely on horseshit tricks" is -- well, frankly -- very probably horseshit."<br /><br />"Making a plausible-looking fake email from Admiral Ackbar. For a talk. For my job."<br /><br />"I think traces of a Baltimore accent are creeping into my vowels. I'm like an impressionable 14-year-old girl. It's tree-ew."<br /><br />"Developing a rash whenever you shave more than twice in five days seems like a sure sign of chronic hobo-ism."<br /><br />"Local TV news is like a refrigerator that I know is empty, but which I inexplicably keep checking anyhow."<br /><br />"Emo boys are a little like Jenga; remove the spikey belt and key ring, and everything falls apart in a sad little pile."<br /><br />"Just insinuated an adorable stuffed bear into the crook of Ellie's right arm. Crazy-cute, but admittedly a bit "Weekend at Bernie's."  (Ellie is his infant daughter)<br /><br />"The Grammy awards must be what it's like when boring people do mushrooms."<br /><br />"Just referred to a limo as "The Hoopty Noir." Why am I even allowed to type?"<br /><br />So I'm digging Merlin's 'twits', but I can't imagine following more than one or two people and I REALLY can't imagine getting updates via SMS.
