Michael Wesch is an anthropologist with an interest in the Internet.  He posted an interesting video a while back (<a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6gmP4nk0EOE">The Machine is Using Us</a>) that got quite a bit of attention.<br /><br />I just saw (via <a href="http://battellemedia.com">John Battelle's Blog</a>) <a href="http://feeds.feedburner.com/%7Er/JohnBattellesSearchblog/%7E3/356806060/004558.php">a video of him presenting at the Library of Congress</a>.  It is interesting to see him analyze and reflect on some of the changes that have occurred in the last few years, and how it is changing our world. <br /><br />As someone who rarely spends much time watching video on the small screen, I enjoyed all of this 55 minute video.
